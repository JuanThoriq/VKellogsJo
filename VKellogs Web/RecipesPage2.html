Breakfast Pop-Tarts®
Parfait

Fresh fruit, yogurt, peanut butter and Pop-
Tarts® get your day off to a delicious start.

10 minutes 3 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups plain low-fat yogurt
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 Kellogg's® Pop-Tarts® Brown Sugar
    Cinnamon toaster pastries
  • 1 1/2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1 Frozen non-dairy whipped topping,
    thawed (optional)

Directions

  1. In small bowl whisk together
    yogurt, peanut butter and maple
    syrup.
  2. In three 12-ounce parfait glasses
    alternately layer yogurt mixture,
    KELLOGG'S® POP-TARTS toaster pastry
    pieces and strawberries. Top with
    whipped topping (if desired).
